Outcome 3.2 - Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency


Apportioning time for research and defining the key concepts of the website such as design, content and timeframe may help the efficiency of the process and productivity. If the concept is clear this facilitates breaking down the overall goal into specific tasks and objectives. These can be divided into small and large tasks. Each time should have a realistic estimate of the duration it may take to complete the task. Therefore, can set clear, achievable objectives each day that will allow them to finish the task according to the deadline. To improve productivity and efficiency, it is also important to take breaks. One can not maintain a given level of concentration throughout prolonged periods and so breaks are necessary to keep one engaged and stimulated. If it is possible to delegate tasks to an appropriate person, then this is a means to increase productivity and efficiency too. In addition, it is important that appropriate support channels are available to ensure that if an individual requires help or assistance to complete a task, they are able to acquire this help within a timely manner to reduce the negative impact on the completion of a task. Ensuring that you and/or collaborators have the correct tools, equipment and working environment prior to the start of the task is also important to maximise productivity and efficiency. In turn, reducing distractions such as irrelevant google searches or social media visits can also improve productivity and efficiency.

Outcome 3.4 - Describe how you would go about testing digital solutions


There are various aspects to consider when testing a digital solution. These include having a clearly defined demographic for the digital platform. Different age groups, individual's located in different geographical locations and of different ages may have different access to devices and connection to the internet so one would have to bear this in mind when developing a solution. Also the performance of the digital solution may be affected if it has not been tested on different browsers and/or different devices. It is important to test the functionality of the platform too such as the capability for the user to reset the password if required, printing, copy/pasting of the page and the storage/processing of user information. First and foremost I would define, plan and document the testing and quality assurance process. I would possess a quality management plan, an optional document that defines the acceptable level of quality of the product and details how the project will achieve this requirement. I would ensure that all testing criteria are met and then seek individuals to test the platform. One method could be to outsource the testing to software engineers or users on github whereby essential modifications may be identified and made.
