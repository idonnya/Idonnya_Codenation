About Me

Hi all. I am Idonnya. I recently finished my PhD in Musculoskeletal Sciences at UCL and am awaiting my PhD viva (thank you covid-19!). I also studied my MSC I and II at The CRI (Paris Descartes & Paris Diderot) whereby I was afforded the opportunity to work on various projects globally including UFRJ in Rio de Janeiro and the MIT, Boston, USA. Despite having a training in medical sciences and medicine, I am deeply interested in the law and am tranisition from academia to commercial (or human rights law). To aid my progression, especially with regards to commerce and banking, I want to learn and develop my coding skills to marry well with my abilities in statistics. I am of proud mixed heritage: Jamaican, Nigerian and Black British.


My Interests
